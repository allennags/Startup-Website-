Who We Are

About PutoShop

PutoShop is a local startup born in the heart of Bayambang, Pangasinan. We started with one goal: bring real, homemade puto to every table — at a price that makes sense.

We're not a big corporation. We're students who believe in the craft, in the product, and in the community that supports us. Every piece of puto we make carries that weight.

From the first batch we ever steamed to the hundreds of orders we've fulfilled, the mission has never changed — make it fresh, make it right, and make it worth every peso.
